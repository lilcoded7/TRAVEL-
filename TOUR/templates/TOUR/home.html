ABOUT INDIA EVISA

Indian eVisa is an Electronic Travel Authorization to enter India. India offers evisa for more than 100+ countries. Travelers can aply for evisa online and recieved electronic travel authrozation in PDF format. Upon arrival in India traveler can show the printed copy of the visa letter and get their passport stamped. 

India Goverment offers multiple visa option e-Tourist Visa, e-Medical Visa and e-Business Visa for India. The applicants form eligible country can apply for any of these e-Visa categories depending upon their purpose of visit to India. E-Visa process is complete online as no paperwork is required and Indian e-visa will be sent via email. Applicants have to carry a print out of e-visa and passport while travelling which will be verified by Indian officer at immigration and will get stamp on the passport on the basis of Indian e-visa.

About e-Tourist Visa

Tourist may apply for Indian tourist visa anytime  for 30 days, 1 Year or 5 Year and arrive at selected Indian airport and their continuous stay during each visit shall not exceed 30 or 90 days for countries except USA, UK, Canada and Japan. For USA , UK, Canada and Japan continuous stay during each visit shall not exceed 180 days from the date of entry in India. Tourists can also avail this facility within 18 to 24 business hours for urgent travels. This process of issuing an Indian tourist e-visa is entirely online where travellers can fill the visa application form. Approved e-Visa will be sent via email to the email
id which is mentioned by the applicant in the form. The travellers have to submit his/her application on this website, using online application form and pay the visa fee. The traveller will get a confirmation by email after submission of their Indian visa application. The purpose of e-Tourist visa can be recreation or sightseeing or Meeting friends or relatives or short term yoga programme etc. e-Tourist Visa is valid for multiple entries in India, which cannot be extended.

About e-Business Visa

Indian Business Visa is for those who want to travel to India for business purpose. Applicants can have the Indian business e-visa in 18-24hrs and urgent business travellers can get the same in 8-12 hrs under urgent visa. Business visa is available for those travellers who want to set up industries/business ventures, sale/purchase/trade, to recruit man power, participate in exhibition/business meetings, expert or specialist for ongoing project, conducting tours, delivering lectures. Travellers who are travelling on behalf of their companies can also get the services of e-Business Visa.

A bout e-Medical Visa

Indian Medical Visa is available for 60 days. E-Medical Visa should be availed within this period. The purpose of Indian medical e-visa will be short term treatment of SELF or family member along with the patient. This Medical Visa is valid for 3 entries in India, which cannot be extended. Indian medical visa doesnâ€™t require any visit to the embassy at your home country. Customer can simply fill up the exact information over the Indian e-visas website and receive Indian e-visa via email. E-Visa will be delivered to the customer within 24-48 hrs after the application is submitted. Urgent Visa will be delivered within 8-12 hrs but the service fees will be high for the same. As e-Medical Visas are provided by Indian Government itself so, only short term medical trips are permitted to save your time and money.

Eligibility

1. International Travelers whose sole objective of visiting India is recreation , sight-seeing , casual visit to meet friends or relatives, short duration medical treatment or casual business visit.
2. Passport should have at least six months validity from the date of arrival in India. The passport should have at least two blank pages for stamping by the Immigration Officer.
3. International Travelers should have return ticket or onward journey ticket, with sufficient money to spend during his/her stay in India.
4. International Travelers having Pakistani Passport or Pakistani origin may please apply for regular Visa at Indian Mission.

FREQUENTALY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is India eVisa ?

Answer. Indian eVisa is an Electronic Travel Authorization to enter India. India offers evisa for more than 169+ countries. Travelers can aply for evisa online and recieved electronic travel authrozation in PDF format. Upon arrival in India traveler can show the printed copy of the visa letter and get their passport stamped.

Am I eligible for Indian Visa ?

Answer. India offers eVisa to more than 169+ countries. Click here to check if your country is eligible for India visa.  You must look for the name of the country as on your passport and not the country where you live. If you are unable to find your country in the India evisa eligible country list, you can still apply for India visa by visiting nearest Indian embassy in person.

Has India Lifted Travel Restriction ?

Answer. Yes, India has no opened its border for foreign Tourism. India welcomes tourist from most of the country for Tourism, Business & Medical travel. Traveller can obtain following visa to travel to India. 30 Days Tourist eVisa, Business Evisa & Medical eVisa.

Is it safe to travel India ?

Answer. Yes, India has vaccinated more than 100Cr people and has almost no deaths due to Covid in last few months. Travelling to India is safe however all travellers are strictl advised to follow all covid guidlines.

How can I apply for India eVisa ?

Answer. You can apply for India visa by filling up a simple & easy application. Once you have filled up application and uploaded your document, we will verify your application and process your visa application. It usually takes 3-4 working days for the Indian eVisa to be approved by Indian Goverment. Once eVisa is approved, it will be emailed to you in PDF format. 

How much Indian eVisa  Cost ?

Answer. Indian visa fee depends on the ype of visa and nationality of the applicant. The visa fee ranges form USD 26 for 30 days tourist visa to USD 82 for 1 year visa. Our visa fee includes govt visa fee & our service fee. You can apply for visa either through govt portal or on website. Click here know the benefits of applying visa with us.   

How can I apply for 10 Year India eVisa ?

Answer. India offers maximum 5 year tourist eVisa with Multiple Entry option. If you wish to apply for 10 year visa, you must apply for OCI card. You can obtain OCI card by apply online with our easy platform. If you already have exiting valid OCI card, you can travel to Indai without eVisa. Please ensure you check the validity of OCI card before travelling to India. 

How can I get India Visa on arrival ?

Answer. Unfortunately, India does not provide visa on arrival. Before your trip you need to apply and obtain your visa, US citizens who arrive in India without a valid visa will not be allowed to enter. It is alwasy advisable to obtain India eVisa before traveling to India. You can apply for India visa by filling our simple online application. Apply Now. 

What is the Port of Arrival in India ?

Answer. Your port of arrival is the city where you will initially enter India. In case if your travel plan and port of entry has changed, you can still travel to India using the same eVisa letter. Indian eVisa cannot be used to enter India via road. You must arrive in India through only eVisa eligible port of entry. 

How can I apply for expidted India eVisa ?

Answer. As soon as you complete your online eVisa application, our team can process your application throught India govt  without delay however it is alwasy advised to apply for India eVisa 3 days in advance. The approval or rejection of Visa depends on Indian Govt and cannot be affected by any thrid party websites.  

My passport expires in 6 months, can I still apply for India eVisa?

Answer. No, it is mandatory that your passport must be valid for 6 months from date of arrival in India. You must apply for new passport in case  your passport has less than 6 months validity, You must also have two blank pages on your passport for getting the visa stam at the Indian airport.  

I do  not remeber my previous India visa number or travel date?

Answer. If you have travelled India preivously using eVisa and do not remember the visa details, we can help you acces your preivous evisa letter. You must also check your new and old passport for preivous visa number stamped. If you are still unable to find your preivous visa details, you can provide aproximate date, month and year of travel.

I have made mistake in application, how can amend my application ?

Answer. If you have applied for Indian evisa throught our online application, we can help you amend your application. You can only make changein the application if the application has not been processed yet. Once eVisa application is processed, it is not possible to make further changes.

My India eVisa letter has incorrect information ?

Answer. It is extremley important that your Indian eVisa letter should have details exctaly as on passport. In case if details in your visa letter does not match with passport, please contact our team. In case of incorrect information on the visa letter, you must apply for fresh application with correct information. 

What can I do if my Indian eVisa is rejected ?

Answer. If your Indian evisa application has been rejected please contact our team. It is important to find out the exact reason for rejection. If the visa has been rejected due to incorrect information filled in the application, we can still help you obtain new evisa. Please do not fill up multiple application as visa can be rejected due to multiple & redudant application.
